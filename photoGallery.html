Temple of the Scared Tooth, Sri Lanka

The Temple of the Scared Tooth Relic is a world- renowed place of worship,where the left Cannie tooth of Gautum Budda is enshired.

Galle, Sri Lanka

Walk through the colonial-era streets and experience the rich history of Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

Sigiriya Lion Rock is an ancient rock fortress known for its massive column of rock that reaches nearly 200 meters high.

Explore the Scenic Beauty of Ella, Sri Lanka

Experience the stunning views, tea plantations, and the iconic Nine Arches Bridge in Ella, Sri Lanka.

Yala National Park, Sri Lanka

Yala National Park is a huge area of forest, grassland and lagoons bordering the Indian Ocean, in southeast Sri Lanka.

Adam's Peak, Sri Lanka

Adam's Peak is believed to bear the footprint of Lord Buddha.

Marble Beach, Sri Lanka

The name “Marble Beach” is derived from the fact that when the sea is calm on a clear and bright day, the surface can be seen to be shining like marble.
